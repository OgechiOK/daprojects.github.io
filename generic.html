About Me

I am a detail-oriented and results-driven Data Analyst skilled in using Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Power BI, PostgreSQL, and Tableau to transform complex datasets into actionable insights. I specialize in creating clear and compelling dashboards and reports to communicate insights to stakeholders, ensuring data integrity and quality through meticulous data cleaning and preprocessing.

I collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams to identify key metrics and develop robust reporting systems that support informed decision-making. My analytical skills, combined with a strong attention to detail, enable me to proactively identify trends and patterns, uncovering hidden opportunities for business growth.

Holding a medical degree (MD), I bring a unique perspective that bridges clinical knowledge with data-driven insights. I am passionate about contributing to innovative projects and helping organizations make data-driven decisions that foster growth and efficiency.

In addition to my professional pursuits, I enjoy crafting beaded clothing accessories, watching sitcoms, and exploring different genres of music. While I love all animals, I have a special fondness for cats.

If you're interested in discussing data analysis projects, have questions about my work, or would like to explore potential collaborations, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm eager to connect and explore how we can achieve insightful and impactful results together.
